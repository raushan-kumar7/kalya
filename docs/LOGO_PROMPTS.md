# Logo Design Prompts for Kalya

Based on the brand strategy in [BRAND.md](file:///D:/Projects/personal/kalya/docs/BRAND.md) and [OVERVIEW.md](file:///D:/Projects/personal/kalya/docs/OVERVIEW.md), **Kalya** (Sanskrit for *healthy, fit, or ready for tomorrow*) represents a calm, trustworthy financial command center. 

Its key visual metaphor is a **minimalist leaf that doubles as an ascending graph line or a compass pointer pointing top-right**, symbolizing organic growth ("Vriksha") and the upward trend of net worth.

The primary color scheme features:
* **Deep Emerald Green** (`#0D3E26` - stability, trust, organic growth)
* **Satin Gold** (`#D4AF37` / `#B8912E` - prosperity, premium quality, future goal milestones)
* **Alabaster Off-White** (`#F9F8F6` - clean canvas, calm space)

## 2. DALL-E 3 Prompts

DALL-E 3 excels at adhering strictly to text instructions and generating clean, isolated graphic elements.

### Option A: Clean Vector Logo (Isolated on White)
> **Prompt:** A clean, flat vector logo for a premium Indian personal finance app named 'Kalya'. The logo should feature a minimalist, abstract icon of a single leaf that cleverly incorporates a rising line chart or graph pointing towards the top right. Use a color palette of deep emerald green (#0D3E26) and satin gold (#D4AF37). The design must be isolated on a solid off-white (#F9F8F6) background, with no text, no realistic shadows, and no mockups. Elegant, professional, and trustworthy.

### Option B: Typographic Wordmark with Icon
> **Prompt:** A modern typographic logo for a finance brand. The text 'kalya' is written in a clean, lowercase, sophisticated sans-serif font. The letter 'y' or 'l' subtly transitions into a minimalist leaf pointing upwards and to the right. The color of the text is deep emerald green, with the leaf accent in a matte gold color. Placed on a clean alabaster background. Minimal, premium, vector style.

## Visual Concept Reference

```mermaid
graph TD
    A[Sanskrit: Kalya / Fit & Ready] --> B[Leaf: Growth & Vitality]
    A --> C[Compass: Direction & Readiness]
    A --> D[Graph: Ascending Net Worth]
    
    B & C & D --> E[The Icon: Stylized Leaf + Top-Right Ascent]
    
    E --> F[Deep Emerald Green #0D3E26]
    E --> G[Satin Gold Accent #D4AF37]
    
    Style E fill:#0D3E26,stroke:#D4AF37,stroke-width:2px,color:#fff
```

> [!TIP]
> When executing these prompts, you can experiment with adding the sub-text "finance" or "ready for tomorrow" in small, elegant type below the icon, but generally, starting with the **icon-only** prompt yields the cleanest logo results that can be easily paired with the brand fonts (*Outfit* and *Inter*) later.
